Pact

Pact is an upcoming action-adventure game that takes in a bright fantasy world. Several factions are fighting for control resources and secrets to determine the future of the land.

Who will thrive in this dangerous world? Who will fall into obscurity? Who will recieve your pact?

Game Systems

Pact uses systemetized design to create a living, breathing world. Each of the major factions vie for control of several villages—the lifeblood of survival in Pact. Each village will explore, expand, rise, and fall without influence from the player. This enables emergent gameplay, where quests and story elements evolve based on the unique history of your world. Bring your favored faction to dominance, or maintain the balance of power to ensure peace. Participate in raids to take land by force, forage for rare materials, secure trade routes and the flow of wealth, or starve a villge into submission. The choice is yours.

The following is a list of planned systems for Pact, it is not comprehensive:

  • A gathering system to find exotic resources hidden throughout the land.
  • An item system to customize your character with different weapons and armor.
  • A simulated economy that dynamically determines the price of goods based on supply and demand.
  • A stances system to broadcast peaceful, aggressive, and stealthy intent for the player and NPCs.
  • A fast-paced combat system to take resources and territory by force.
  • A relationship system that ranges from nefarious enemy to treasured ally.
  • A construction system for villages to show off their wealth and resources with special buildings.
  • A jobs system that dynamically upates villagers' jobs and schedules based on the needs of the village.
  • An outpost system for villages to claim land, fortify outposts, and launch raids automatically.
