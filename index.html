Follow for Updates

The name is constructed from two old english words; it combines "Ƿyrceþ" (it works) and "þrang" (throng or crowd) with "Áspyre" (investigate, study discover). This embodies an unconventional approach constructing experimental games using systems that yield novel emergent experiences.

You can call this the work-throng. Our current project is Pact, a system-driven action RPG set in a bright fantasy world.
