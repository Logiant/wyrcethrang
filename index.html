Ongoing Projects

My current project is a third-person RPG with political and citybuiler elements with an emphasis on cosistent worldbuilding. It's heavily inspired by the village and politics systems of Pine and the nemesis system in Shadow of Mordor. The ultimate objective is to develop a true living world that will exist and evolve around the player. I'm working on the minimum viable prototype now, which consists of two unfriendly villages on a small island. I post updates on twitter when I get breaks from graduate work.
